Getting Started in 3 Simple Steps

Go from zero to your first AI-tailored application in just a few minutes. Here’s how to set up Taylor for success.

1

Get Your API Key

Taylor is powered by Google's Gemini AI. To unlock its capabilities, you'll need a free API key from Google AI Studio. This is your personal key to the AI engine.

Get Free API Key
2

Install & Configure

Add the Taylor extension to your Chrome browser. Once installed, open it and paste in your API key. This one-time setup securely stores your key and creates your profile.

Add to Chrome
3

Build Your Master Resume

Inside the extension, fill out your profile with your complete work history, education, projects, and skills. Think of this as your "master resume"—the single source of truth Taylor will use to craft perfectly tailored documents for any job you apply to.

You're all set! Now visit any job site and let Taylor work its magic.
