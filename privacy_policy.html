Privacy Policy for Taylor Extension

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Taylor (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and disclose information, particularly the resume data you provide when using our Extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

2. Information We Collect and How It's Stored

When you use our Extension to create and manage your resumes, the information you input, which may include your name, contact details, work experience, education, skills, and other professional information ("Resume Data"), is collected.

This Resume Data is stored on secure servers managed by us, utilizing Google Cloud's Firebase services, specifically Firestore, as our backend database. We store your data to enable the core functionality of the Extension, such as allowing you to save, access, edit, and manage your resumes.

As the developers of this Extension, we have the technical capability to access the Resume Data stored in the Firestore database.

3. How We Use Your Information

The primary purpose for collecting and storing your Resume Data is to provide you with the services offered by the Extension. This includes:

  • Allowing you to create, store, view, edit, and manage your resumes.
  • Enabling the functionality required for the Extension to operate as intended.

Developer access to your Resume Data is strictly limited. We will only access your Resume Data if it is essential for:

  • Providing technical support at your request.
  • Troubleshooting issues with the Extension or its underlying services (e.g., Firestore).
  • Maintaining and improving the service's integrity and security.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

We will not use the content of your resumes for any other purposes, such as marketing or analysis, without your explicit prior consent.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal Resume Data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Google Cloud's Firebase services (including Firestore for database and Firebase Authentication) for data storage, user authentication, and hosting infrastructure. Google is responsible for the security of its cloud infrastructure and is bound by its own privacy policies and data protection terms.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your Resume Data if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, prevent fraud, act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of the Extension, or protect against legal liability.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your Resume Data. We utilize Firebase services (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which implement industry-standard security measures to protect data from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access or disclosure. These measures include data encryption in transit (e.g., using HTTPS) and at rest, and secure access controls provided by Google Cloud.

While we and our service providers take reasonable precautions to protect your information, please be aware that no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. User Rights and Choices

You have control over your Resume Data within the Extension. You can:

  • Access and Review: Access and review your Resume Data at any time through the Extension's interface.
  • Edit and Update: Modify or update your Resume Data as needed through the Extension's interface.
  • Delete: Delete your Resume Data through the Extension's interface. Deleting your resume data via the extension will remove it from our active Firestore database.

If you wish to delete your account and all associated Resume Data from our servers, please contact us at valluruchetanreddy@gmail.com or use any 'Delete Account' feature if made available within the Extension's settings. Upon request for account deletion, your data will be processed for deletion as outlined in the Data Retention section.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your Resume Data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Extension's services.

If you delete your account or request deletion of your data, we will take steps to delete your Resume Data from our active databases within 30 days, unless we are legally required to retain it for a longer period (for example, to comply with legal, tax, or accounting requirements). Anonymized or aggregated data, which does not identify you personally, may be retained for analytical purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at valluruchetanreddy@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and by posting a notice on the Extension's Chrome Web Store listing or within the Extension itself. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
valluruchetanreddy@gmail.com
